	Plugin for accessing open data published by the Cartographic and Geological Institute of Catalonia (Catalan mapping agency).
    Includes spatial toponymic searches, streets, roads, coordinates in different reference systems and load of WMS base layers of Catalonia.
	This plugin uses suds-py3 and wsse libraries.
